Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how AutoPost (the "Service") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our video auto-posting application for TikTok. By using the Service, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

Account information

When you connect your TikTok account, we receive information through TikTok's official authorization flow, such as your TikTok user ID, display name, and profile information you authorize us to access, along with access tokens that allow us to post on your behalf.

Content you provide

We collect the videos, captions, and scheduling settings you upload to the Service so we can publish them to TikTok at the times you choose.

Usage information

We may collect basic technical data such as log data, device information, and information about how you interact with the Service, to operate and improve it.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To connect to your TikTok account and publish your videos as scheduled;
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Service;
  • To communicate with you about your account and support requests;
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues or abuse;
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. TikTok Data and API Use

Our use of information received from TikTok APIs adheres to TikTok's Developer Terms and Policies, including any limitations on the use and transfer of such data. We use TikTok access tokens only to perform the actions you have requested, such as publishing scheduled videos. We do not use TikTok data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only:

  • with TikTok, as necessary to publish your content and operate the integration;
  • with service providers who help us run the Service (for example, hosting and storage), under confidentiality obligations;
  • if required by law, regulation, or valid legal process;
  • to protect the rights, safety, and property of AutoPost or others.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. When you disconnect your TikTok account or delete your account, we delete the associated access tokens and remove your content, except where we are required to retain data to comply with legal obligations.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You can disconnect your TikTok account at any time through TikTok's settings or within AutoPost, which revokes our ability to post on your behalf. You may also request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Your continued use of the Service after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@example.com.
